MyProject: Resolution versus Precision in X-ray detection of Pneumonia

Zanchen Li

zanchen.li@duke.edu

Paper PDF

In this paper we try to explore the relationship between resolutions and precisions in X-ray detection. I design a 3 convolution layers network and run different sized images through the network and found that while recall rate of the algorithm increases with the size of the image, the precision decreases with the size of the image. I also try a different method, fourier transform and multiply with low pass filter, to blur the image and achivevd a similar result.
